About Us

Our team of scientists and engineers are using innovative lab and AI technologies to combat fibrotic diseases. Starting with cardiac fibrosis, we combine high-throughput screening, advanced imaging, and agentic AI to discover and refine therapeutic compounds. Our agentic lab-in-the-loop system learns from our best in class experimental assay to improve drug predictions over time. Our mission? To improve patient outcomes for those with fibrotic diseases.
